What is NXTGENAI?

NXTGENAI is an AI-powered marketplace with 23+ tools and autonomous agents designed to automate content creation, business operations, marketing, and growth. One platform for all your AI needs.

How does the credit system work?

Basic tools cost 2 credits, premium tools 3, AI agents 5. Your plan includes monthly credits (100 for Starter, 500 for Business) that refresh each cycle.

Can I try before I buy?

Yes! We offer free-tier tools including Caption Generator, Hashtag Generator, Content Ideas, and Email Reply Assistant. No credit card needed.

What's the difference between tools and agents?

Tools are single-purpose generators (input → output). AI Agents are conversational and autonomous — they handle multi-step workflows across domains like SEO, sales, and support.

Yes. Custom Business Automation includes 3,000–10,000+ credits/month, dedicated account manager, custom integrations, and SLA guarantees.

We use HttpOnly cookies, CSRF protection, role-based access control, encrypted data, and maintain 99.9% uptime SLA with enterprise-grade security.
